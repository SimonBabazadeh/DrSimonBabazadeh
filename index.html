About Me

I am a passionate dentist dedicated to providing exceptional dental care with a gentle touch. My philosophy centers around patient education and preventive care to help patients maintain optimal oral health throughout their lives.

After graduating Dental Medicine with honors from UMF Cluj-Napoca, I completed advanced training at Folktandvården Västra Götaland before moving on to my journey as a private dentist year 2020.
